Wubba Duckies

You're chillin' in your bath. What are you missing?
A Wubba Duckie, of course!.
Browse and order from a variaty of Wubba Ducks and while you're at it, rate em. Beccause, why not?

Wubba Duckies was created to be a eCommerce site, selling rubber ducks.
It was my first experience using the Ruby On Rails framework guided through with my online bootcamp, CareerFoundry, a complex course that taught a lot and also packed a lot in.

Framework and Languages:

  • HTML5
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • Bootstrap
  • Ruby on Rails

Features include:
being fully responsive on all devices, functioning contact forms, search bar, payment forms, zoom function, comments, rating system and a sign in process.

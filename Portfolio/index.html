About

I am a web developer from London, England, living in Jersey City.

I took the plunge into this field two years ago by taking as many online courses as I could. Starting with the freebies I found online, to a couple of paid extensive online boot-camps. Since then I have honed my skills and today showcase my portfolio website. I have been a free lance web developer for a short period and am now looking for a full-time career doing what I love. In the projects section you'll find some examples of my work.

Problem-solving is second nature to me and fits perfectly with my passion for coding. My strong belief in clear communication allows me to work well professionally and to build dependable relationships, not only in the work place but in my daily life.
My professional skills include: HTML5, CSS, javaScript, the use of Bootstrap and Ruby on Rails framework.
I have a relentless drive to learn and take any chance I can to continue doing so.
